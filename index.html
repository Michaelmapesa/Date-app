About Us

Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born. Luckily for us,
there are ways to calculate the day of the week from a specific date.And result come in relation to the Akan name of Ghana community,
below are brief explanation of the names:

Male Names

  1. Sunday-Kwasi
  2. Monday-Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday-Kwabena
  4. Wednesday-Kwaku
  5. Thursday-Yaw
  6. Friday-Koti
  7. Saturday-Kwame

Female Names

  1. Sunday-Akosua
  2. Monday-Adwoa
  3. Tuesday-Abenaa
  4. Wednesday-Akua
  5. Thursday-Yaa
  6. Friday-Afua
  7. Saturday-Ama
